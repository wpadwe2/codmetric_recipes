Grilled Lemon Chicken

Ingredients:

  • Full Chicken
  • 1 lemon (juiced)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Mix lemon juice, olive oil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Marinate chicken for 30 minutes.
  3. Grill for 6-7 minutes per side until fully cooked.
  4. Serve hot with a side salad.

Honey Garlic Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken thighs
  • 3 tbsp honey
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Steps:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan and cook chicken.
  2. Mix honey, garlic, and soy sauce.
  3. Pour sauce over chicken and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Serve hot with rice.

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup melted butter

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix all ingredients together.
  3. Pour into a greased baking pan.
  4. Bake for 30 minutes.
  5. Let cool and serve.

Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oil

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Scramble eggs and remove from pan.
  3. Stir-fry vegetables, then add rice and soy sauce.
  4. Mix eggs back in and cook for 2 more minutes.
  5. Serve hot.
